About the Project

This project leverages advanced machine learning techniques to recognize handwritten flowcharts, allowing for automated data analysis and visualization. Explore our application to experience the magic of automated recognition.

How it Works

Our application processes and interprets handwritten flowcharts through state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms. Here's a simple overview:

  • Upload your handwritten flowchart image.
  • Click the "Detect" button.
  • Our system will analyze the image and identify objects and their connections.
  • View and download the detected flowchart.
